# Test Questions for the REIT Advisor Agent

## Portfolio Analytics Questions

1. What is the current portfolio occupancy rate?
2. How many properties are in the portfolio and what is the total square footage?
3. What are the top 5 tenants by annual rent?
4. Show me the geographic distribution of properties by state.
5. What is the average cap rate by property type?
6. Which properties are currently vacant?
7. How many leases expire in the next 12 months?
8. What percentage of tenants are investment-grade rated?
9. What is the total annual rent from triple-net leases?
10. Show me the tenant concentration - which tenants represent more than 5% of total rent?

## Financial Performance Questions

11. What was the total Net Operating Income for fiscal year 2024?
12. How has same-store NOI growth trended over the past 3 years?
13. What is the average occupancy rate by fiscal quarter for 2024?
14. What were the total capital expenditures in 2023 vs 2024?
15. Show me FFO and AFFO trends quarterly from 2022 to 2025.
16. Which properties have the highest NOI?
17. What is the rent collection rate trend?

## Market Performance Questions

18. What is the current dividend yield and how does it compare to the 10-year Treasury?
19. How has the stock price performed year-to-date?
20. What is the Price/FFO multiple trend over the past 2 years?
21. How does Realty Income's total return compare to the S&P 500?
22. What is the current debt-to-EBITDA ratio and how has it trended?
23. What is the payout ratio and is it sustainable?
24. Show me the dividend per share growth trajectory.

## Risk Assessment Questions

25. Which properties have the highest risk scores?
26. What is the breakdown of risk levels across the portfolio?
27. Which tenants pose the greatest credit risk?
28. Show me properties with high lease expiration risk in the next year.
29. What is the average risk score by property type?
30. How does geographic risk vary by state?

## Document & Qualitative Questions

31. What was discussed in the most recent earnings call?
32. What is Realty Income's European expansion strategy?
33. Explain the Spirit Realty Capital merger and its impact.
34. What are the key risk factors mentioned in the 10-K filing?
35. What is a triple-net lease and why does Realty Income use them?
36. What are Realty Income's ESG goals?
37. How does the company manage interest rate risk?
38. What is FFO and why is it important for REIT investors?

## Complex Multi-Tool Questions

39. Given the current occupancy rate and lease expiration schedule, what is the revenue risk over the next 2 years?
40. Compare the financial performance of retail vs industrial properties and explain the strategic rationale for diversification.
41. What is the relationship between tenant credit quality and property risk scores?
42. Based on the latest earnings call and financial data, is the dividend sustainable?
43. How does the portfolio's geographic concentration align with market risk assessments?
